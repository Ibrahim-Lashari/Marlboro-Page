MARLBORO

Marlboro Math League

  • Math league is a club where students compete against other local schools
    in monthly math competitions over the course of 6 months as members of the
    Shore Math League. The top schools receive a trophy and the top scoring
    students win cash prizes as well as a trophy to acknowledge their
    accomplishments. The math league also participates in a second monthly
    math competition against other schools in the state as part of the NJ
    Math League.
